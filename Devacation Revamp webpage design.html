The Mission

"Devacation Revamp" isn't just a hackathon; it's a creative retreat for the digital architect. We believe that innovation flourishes when the mind is at ease. Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-intensity coding and the rejuvenating spirit of a vacation. Refresh your perspective, revamp your skills, and build the future.
